Financial Organisation Cabling Infrastucture

Financial Organisation

This financial organization is one of India's leading capital markets company providing securities broking and advisory services. It also provides depository services, equity research services and IPO distribution to its clients and offers commodities trading through a separate company. These services are provided both through on-line and off-line distribution channels. It is a pioneer of on-line securities trading in India. Its in-house trading platform is one of the fastest and most efficient trading platforms in the country.

Size of Project

  • Users: Around 5000 Edge
  • Switches: More than 100
  • Core Switches: Two 7 slot chassis
  • Routers

Challenges

  • The organization being a Stock broking firm required its IT Infrastructure to work on a 24 x 7 x 4 basis
  • Broadcast and Virus attack Challenges
  • Remote branches across the country Connecting to the location
  • High security a must for both Wired and Wireless Users
  • Hundreds of servers

Implementation

  • The network consisted of around 5000 users across 10 floors of a building. Each floor has 8 Ethernet switches stacked together with 2 uplinks of fiber going to two central core switches to maintain redundancy.
  • The core switches are configured at High availability mode using VRRP.
  • Every floor is configured to be in a different VLAN.

Summary

A fully redundant and secured network with 100% Uptime.
