Managed Services

Managed Services

At Emarson, we primarily provide three types of services

Remotely provided services

Through these services we deploy an Engineer to resolve issues online while working remotely and not on actual site.

Onsite Services

We provide an Engineer on the actual site to resolve issues including that of physical nature.

Onsite Permanent Engineer

An engineer is permanently located at the client’s location to be of assistance whenever needed. Thus type of a model is deployed when there is a specific criticality of work.

Overview

We truly understand that your infrastructure is your lifeline. We help reduce costs while connecting you to your customers and also the suppliers. With digital transformation IT organizations are coping up with a lot of changes in the IT environment. Right from new technologies to preparing the work-force to adapt these new technologies, also adjusting to new security protocols to compliances, the whole shift has been overwhelming. Emarson steps into making this change bearable and comfortable and making it easy for assimilation into your organization. We manage your IT from day-to-day infrastructure to all the operational activities in order to help you understand the changing IT landscape. With technical expertise and experience we help you reduce costs at all levels and bring in more profitability.

"We help you grow by letting you achieve the following objectives by way of providing super technical services"

Rapid problem solving

With the objective of avoiding network downtime, we help quickly identify issues and streamline incident management processes. This also helps improve IT service levels.

Mitigating risks

For uninterrupted business, Emarson deploys proactive tools combined with their technical experts. This mitigates risks largely.

Operational efficiencies

With automated processes and anticipative management increase your operational efficiencies while reducing costs as you need fewer support resources.
